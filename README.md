# OnePointHub

**OnePointHub** is an open-source, modular business management platform designed to serve as a one-stop solution for all your business needs. Whether you're managing HR, payroll, files, support tickets, or knowledge bases — OnePointHub brings it all together in a unified and elegant interface.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🌐 **Modular Architecture** – Enable or disable features based on your business needs.
- 👨‍💼 **HR Management** – Employee records, departments, designations, attendance.
- 💵 **Payroll Module** – Payroll generation, salary history, payslips.
- 🧾 **Accounting** *(Coming Soon)* – Ledgers, transactions, invoices, reports.
- 📂 **File Management** *(Coming Soon)* – Secure file storage and sharing.
- 🎫 **Ticketing System** *(Coming Soon)* – Customer support tools and workflows.
- 📘 **Knowledge Base** *(Coming Soon)* – Document your internal or external knowledge.
- ⚙️ **Settings & Roles** – Customizable roles, permissions, and configurations.
